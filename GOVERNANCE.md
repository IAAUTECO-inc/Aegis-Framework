# Project Governance

## 1. Decision Making
AEGIS is governed by the **IA_AUT_ECO Infrastructure Division**. Decisions regarding the core architecture, security protocols, and compliance alignment are final and centralized to ensure the integrity of the sovereign stack.

## 2. Maintainership
Maintainers are appointed based on their history of high-integrity technical contributions and their understanding of the European regulatory landscape (NIS2, AI Act).

## 3. Transparency
While the core roadmap is managed by IA_AUT_ECO, all security audits and compliance reports will be made public to maintain the "Chain of Trust" with our institutional partners.
