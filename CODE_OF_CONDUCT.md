# Code of Conduct: Engineering Integrity

## 1. Our Pledge
As contributors and maintainers of the AEGIS Framework, we pledge to prioritize **systemic resilience, transparency, and auditability** over rapid deployment. Our goal is to foster an environment of high-level technical excellence for sovereign AI and inclusive robotics.

## 2. Our Standards
* **Technical Rigor:** Every contribution must be justified by its alignment with the AEGIS doctrine (deterministic, local, and auditable).
* **Intellectual Honesty:** Acknowledge the limitations and potential failure modes of any proposed algorithmic change.
* **Professionalism:** Focus on peer-review and technical merit. We value cold, objective criticism of code to ensure the safety of end-users (people with disabilities).

## 3. Enforcement
The IA_AUT_ECO Infrastructure Division oversees the application of these standards. Behaviors that compromise the security or the institutional reputation of the project will result in immediate exclusion from the repository.
